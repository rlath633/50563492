About Riget Zoo Adventures

Riget Zoo Adventures (RZA) is a Zoo & Hotel offering multiple attractions and facilities for everyone to enjoy! RZA is also a multipurpose organisation, bringing endangered or unhealthy species from the wild and giving them a free-reign sancurary best suited for each type of animal, aswell as caring and monitering their health to either be kept here to live a long and happy life, or to be rehabilitated back to the wild. RZA was founded by Rosie Riget-Latham in 1989, which started as a small Animal Sancurary for endangered wildlife, and later in 1992 opened as a Safari-Style Zoo, being one of the first in the United Kingdom. Now today, Riget Zoo Adventure is now homing over 100 different species of animals from all over the world, and has over 120 different facilities and attractions for everyone to enjoy, aswell in the last decade, The Hotel opened, offering Safari-Themed rooms, clubhouses, swimming pools and much more.

About the Zoo

At Riget Zoo Adventures, we bring the wonders of the wild to you through our unique safari-style zoo experience. Situated in the heart of nature, our zoo is dedicated to creating unforgettable encounters with a diverse range of exotic animals. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a family looking for a fun day out, or a group seeking an educational adventure, Riget Zoo Adventures has something for everyone.

Our mission at Riget Zoo Adventures is to inspire a love for wildlife and conservation through engaging and immersive experiences. We believe in the power of education and exploration to foster a deep connection between people and the natural world. By promoting awareness and understanding of endangered species and their habitats, we aim to contribute to the global effort of preserving biodiversity for future generations.

Safari Tours: Embark on an exciting safari tour led by experienced guides who will introduce you to our collection of rare and beautiful animals, including lions, giraffes, elephants, and more. Animal Encounters: Experience hands-on interactions with some of our friendliest residents and learn fascinating facts about their lives in the wild. Educational Exhibits: Dive deep into the world of wildlife conservation through our engaging exhibits, which highlight the importance of protecting biodiversity and preserving natural ecosystems. Adventure Playground: Let your little ones unleash their wild side at our adventure playground, complete with jungle-themed play structures and activities.

Ready to embark on a safari adventure like no other? Plan your visit to Riget Zoo Adventures today! Explore our ticket options, check our opening hours, and discover the exciting events and programs we have in store for you. Join us in celebrating the beauty and diversity of the animal kingdom at Riget Zoo Adventures, where every visit is an unforgettable journey into the wild!
